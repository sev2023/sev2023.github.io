Relax in the lap of nature

This trail is well maintained and flat the majority of the way so it is suitable for the whole family. There are numerous parks and lovely waterfalls along the way. It's a great route in the city to relax, stretch your legs, digest lunch or dinner, or just get back out to the peaceful realm of nature. Get that body moving, the lungs breathing in that beautiful fresh air and feel that connection with nature.

More about staying

The following accommodation is situated on or near the Dodder river. Many hosts will collect and deliver you to parts of the route if given prior notice. There is a variety of accommodation - from large country houses to smaller Bed & Breakfast houses to family farms and self catering hostels. Prices/ number of beds/ availability etc. will vary and the walker is strongly advised to make a booking in advance, particularly in the summer months.

Arus Mhuire House

Arus Mhuire B&B is situated on the bank of Dodder river in Dublin's quiet south side. With amazing and friendly hosts, you will be delighted with your stay at Arus Mhuire B&B.

Located just twenty minutes from Dublin City Centre by bus. Arus Mhuire is also the perfect location to explore the wonders of Dublin away from the noise of the city!

Herbert Park Hotel

Herbert Park Hotel is located in Ballsbridge, a neighbourhood in Dublin, and is near a train station and near the beach. The area's natural beauty can be seen at Herbert Park, while National Print Museum and Royal Dublin Society are cultural highlights. Looking to enjoy an event or a match while in town? See what's going on at Aviva Stadium. Spend some time exploring the area's activities, including golfing.
